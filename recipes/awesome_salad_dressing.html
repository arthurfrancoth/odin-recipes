Awesome Salad Dressing Recipe

This is the best salad dressing you will ever taste. Balanced, rich sweet and sour!
It works in every salad combination! Beleive me: you will love it!

The best part is: it only have 5 ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons extra virgen olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white balsamic vinagre
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of Honey
  • Pinch of salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)

Method

This is as simple as it can be!

  1. Place all the ingredients into a mason jar, or similar container.
  2. Put the cover on, and give it a good mix
  3. DONE!
